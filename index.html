Child Safety Standards (CSAE) Policy

App Name: Linkr (Google Play ID: com.levermore.linkr)
Effective Date: 2025-11-01

  1. This App is strictly for adults 18+ only. **Registration requires mandatory ID/Passport OCR age verification** (backend AI validation).
  2. Zero tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) content. **Automated + manual moderation** of all photos and chats.
  3. In-App "Report" button (in Chat/Profile) → 24-hour response time, without leaving the App.
  4. Violation handling: Immediate permanent account ban + content removal + Report to NCMEC (report.cybertip.org) / Cyberspace Administration of China.
